# Daralla Logo Concept

## Primary Direction

The logo should be wordmark-first.

`DARALLA` should appear as a custom or semi-custom geometric wordmark with:

- tight spacing
- crisp cuts
- subtle asymmetry
- a stable horizontal silhouette

The goal is to feel engineered rather than decorative.

## Symbol Direction

A supporting symbol can be used alongside the wordmark for favicons, avatars, and product marks.

Recommended symbol ideas:

### 1. Mirrored D Monogram

Two opposing `D` forms built into a single symmetrical shape.

Why it works:

- feels technical
- scales well
- supports a system-based identity

### 2. D/A Grid Mark

A monogram derived from `D` and `A` within a modular square or circular grid.

Why it works:

- connects to interface logic
- feels precise and modern
- easy to animate

### 3. Signal Ring Emblem

A reduced symbol built from concentric arcs or angular rings.

Why it works:

- aligns with motion and digital systems
- works well in dark UI contexts
- extends naturally into branding motion

## Logo Behavior

- Primary use: wordmark on dark backgrounds
- Secondary use: symbol-only mark for compact surfaces
- Accent use: cyan or green highlights only when needed
- Keep logo treatments flat, minimal, and sharp

## Do Not

- use ornamental serif styling
- use playful rounded marks
- overcomplicate the monogram
- rely on gradients inside the core logo form
