# Daralla Brand Guidelines

## Brand Philosophy

Daralla is a creative technology studio built around clarity, atmosphere, and advanced execution. The brand should feel modern and minimal at first glance, then progressively reveal depth through motion, spatial composition, and system-level detail.

Core principles:

- Precision over noise
- Technology with emotion
- Minimal surfaces, high-impact execution
- Editorial clarity with experimental edge

Daralla should never feel loud or decorative for its own sake. The identity needs to signal a studio that understands both aesthetics and production.

## Color Palette

### Core

- Void Black: `#06080D`
- Deep Slate: `#121722`
- Mist Silver: `#C9D1D9`
- Soft White: `#F4F7FB`

### Accents

- Electric Cyan: `#6EE7F9`
- Signal Green: `#7EF0C7`
- Data Blue: `#3C63FF`

### Usage

- Use dark foundations by default
- Reserve cyan and green for interaction, emphasis, and system highlights
- Use blue for structured interface moments and technical content
- Keep backgrounds restrained and avoid heavy color stacking

## Typography

### Display

Primary feel:

- geometric
- sharp
- engineered

Suggested families:

- Space Grotesk
- Syne
- Sora

Use display typography for:

- headlines
- navigation marks
- hero statements

### Body

Suggested families:

- Manrope
- Inter
- General Sans

Use body typography for:

- case study copy
- system labels
- navigation support text
- dashboards and forms

### Typographic Behavior

- Large headlines with tight tracking
- Uppercase system labels with expanded letter spacing
- Strong contrast between display and body scales
- Generous whitespace around text blocks

## Visual Language

Daralla’s visual system should combine editorial restraint with high-tech atmosphere.

Visual traits:

- dark backgrounds
- subtle grid structures
- fine interface borders
- restrained gradients
- glass or metallic highlights
- radial glows used sparingly
- bold cropping and spatial framing

Graphic motifs:

- data grids
- signal lines
- luminous rings
- scan fields
- modular frames

Motion language:

- smooth transitions
- staged reveals
- directional wipes
- light sweeps
- layered parallax rather than playful bounce

## Brand Voice

Daralla should sound:

- direct
- intelligent
- precise
- contemporary

Avoid:

- hype-heavy wording
- vague creative clichés
- playful brand language that weakens technical credibility

Preferred voice examples:

- "Digital systems with cinematic presence."
- "Built for ambitious launches."
- "Creative technology for high-impact brands."
